Fitness

Being in good physical condition is an important part of military life. Fitness tests are carried out frequently, and being in shape could save the life of someone in a combat role. A fitness test must be taken to get into a service academy. Because fitness is such an important part of my future career, I do my best to stay in shape.

CFA

To get into the United States Air Force Academy or the United States Military Academy, a fitness test must be passed. It's called the Candidate Fitness Assessment, or CFA for short. It consists of a basketball throw from the knees, pull-ups (women have the option of doing a flexed arm hang instead), a 20 ft shuttle run (run 20 feet down and back two times), crunches, push-ups, and a one mile run.
